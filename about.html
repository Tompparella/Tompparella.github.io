About Me

"Who are you again?"

BIO

I'm currently on my third year studying a bachelor's degree on software engineering at LUT-university. 23 years old, I've always been passionate about all things IT, and introduced myself to programming when I was as young as 13. I especially enjoyed making all sorts of games, which I still love doing on my free time.

During my years of studying I've gotten quite comfortable with development work and have learned the most common and useful tools on both the front- and backend, including ReactJS, Node.js, ASP.NET, Express.js, etc. I've also touched on Azure DevOps on my last summer internship at Miradore.

I'm keen on improving my skills very actively, and have been working on a number of personal projects, as well as participating in as many hackathons and code camps that I've had the time for, including the Visma 2021 React Code Camp, and the Finnish Game Jam 2019-2021.

At the moment I am working on my bachelor's thesis about constructing artificial intelligence for video game purposes, especially focusing on simulating economies.
